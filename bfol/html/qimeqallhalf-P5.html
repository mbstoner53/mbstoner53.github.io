Theorem qimeqallhalf-P5 615
Description: Partial Quantified Implication Equivalence Law ( ( E → U ) → U ).

The reverse implication is only true when '𝑥' either does not occur in '𝜑' (qimeqallav-P5 624) or '𝜓' (qimeqallbv-P5 626), or does not occur free in '𝜑' (qimeqalla-P6 705) or '𝜓' (qimeqallb-P6 707).

Assertion
Ref Expression
qimeqallhalf-P5 ⊢ ((∃𝑥𝜑 → ∀𝑥𝜓) → ∀𝑥(𝜑 → 𝜓))
