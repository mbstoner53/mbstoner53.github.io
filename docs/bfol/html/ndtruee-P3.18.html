Theorem ndtruee-P3.18 193
Description: Natural Deduction: '⊤' Elimination Rule.

Rules 17 and 18 allow us to write '( ⊤ → 𝜑)' as a synonym for stating "'𝜑' is true" (or, more precisely, deducable).
